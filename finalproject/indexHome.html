What is ACEs?

The ACEs test is to help provide insight to the adversities that you have faced and to help you understand what you have gone through as a child. ACE stands for Adverse Childhood Experiences. These adversities may impact your lifetime but they do not have to define you. Understanding your childhood traumas can help you understand how to overcome them and how to deal with them when they do affect you.
